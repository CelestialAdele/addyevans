My research

As a member of the HAWC Collaboration, I am interested in the highest-energy photons mankind has detected. Currently, my research pertains to understanding more about the high-energy (GeV and TeV+) emission of globular clusters and, subsequently, millisecond pulsars to understand more about the prospects for detection of annihilating dark matter. I frequently work with Fermi-LAT data as well to answer these questions.
